Our Services

Specialized services in plumbing, bathroom refurbishments, kitchen refurbishments, loft conversions, and home extensions.

Plumbing Services

Complete plumbing solutions: installations, repairs, maintenance, and 24/7 emergency callouts. Expert workmanship for all your plumbing needs including leaks, blockages, boiler repairs, and new installations.

Bathroom Refurbishments

Complete bathroom transformations from design to completion. Full refurbishments including tiling, plumbing, electrical work, and finishing. Create your dream bathroom with our professional team.

Kitchen Refurbishments

Complete kitchen makeovers including design, installation, plumbing, electrical work, and finishing touches. Transform your kitchen into a modern, functional space that suits your lifestyle.

Loft Conversions

Maximize your home's potential with a professional loft conversion. From initial planning and building regulations to construction and finishing, we create additional living space that adds value to your property.

Home Extensions

Single and double-storey extensions to expand your living space. Full project management from planning permission to completion, including foundations, construction, plumbing, and finishing.

Heating & Boiler Services

Boiler installation, servicing, and repairs. Central heating design and installation. Gas Safe registered engineers for all your heating needs, including new system installations and upgrades.
